# Privacy Policy (B2B Foreign Trade Mall) > **Abstract**: We only collect your information when you voluntarily request product consultations, and the sole purpose is to contact you and discuss the details of outsourcing projects.
We adhere to the "minimum necessity" principle. Unlike ordinary B2C e-commerce platforms, we **will not** silently collect your private data when you browse products.
After you click "Consult Now" on your preferred product page and enter your information, the process is as follows: - **Requirement Matching**: Our business team will review your message and match the most suitable technical solution for the outsourcing project. - **One-on-One Manual Follow-up**: We will conduct **one-on-one** communication via the phone number you provided to avoid unsolicited contact. - **Confidentiality Obligation**: Your contact information is only used for our internal sales lead management system and is **strictly prohibited** from being disclosed to any third-party advertisers.
**Reminder**: You do not need to provide any personal information if you are only browsing our outsourcing cases or product list. We only record your contact information when you seek business cooperation with us.
If you have submitted a consultation but no longer wish to be contacted by us, simply inform our staff during the call or send us an email, and we will immediately remove your number from our lead pool.
Your mobile phone number entered on the website is encrypted via TLS during transmission to prevent interception by third parties before it reaches our backend system.
